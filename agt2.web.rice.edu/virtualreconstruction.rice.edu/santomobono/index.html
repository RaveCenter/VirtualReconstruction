Sant Omobono Reconstruction

Our project focused on reconstructing the temple of Sant' Omobono, located near the Forum Boarium in Rome. Given the site's use through multiple chronological phases, we decided to solely focus on reconstructing the first phase of the building (circa 580 BCE). The temple was the first monumental and Tuscanic temple constructed in Central Italy. Due to poor excavation strategies implemented throughout most of the site's excavation, we have hardly any physical remains of the temple itself. Throughout this process, we have extensively used scholarship and contemporaneous comparanda to create our own reconstruction.
