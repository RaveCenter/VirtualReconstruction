Virtual Reconstruction of Historical Cities

Welcome to the Virtual Reconstruction webpage!

This site is a product of collaboration between students and faculty in the Rice University Schools of Humanities, Social Sciences, Architecture, and Engineering. Over the course of a semester three historical sites were constructed in a virtual navigable environment.*

Under the supervision of professors, but working in independent study, student teams researched the archaeology of these sites as well as contemporaneous architecture. They then modeled each building using Rhino3D and Blender before integrating the buildings with the Unity Game Engine.

Enjoy!
